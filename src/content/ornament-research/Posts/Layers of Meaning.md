National ornaments are often treated as stable carriers of inherited meaning. In reality, symbolic meaning is contextual — shaped by social, political, and cultural conditions.
### Context
* Political-symbolic — how flags function in contention (to be avoided as primary frame).
* Ethnographic / ritual textile — rushnik (towel, belt) traditions; motif symbolism (a rich field the research will draw on but not add to).
### Case Study
* Cultural semiotics — Belarusian ornaments as cultural code and national identity (the case study layer).
### Design Method
* Methodological — how AI-mediated participation can generate new meaning

This project shifts focus from past symbolism to present-day interpretation, treating the ornament as a living form capable of absorbing contemporary experience.
