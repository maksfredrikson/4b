The ornamental flag is painted with light in space. Ephemeral, but visible through long-exposure photography. The project emerged from partisan action, where protesters used clothing, flowers, and synchronised demonstrations as coded resistance symbols. It's an impermanent, adaptable intervention that could be concealed or erased when necessary.
